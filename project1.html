    Wireless Hacking - Evil Twin Attack


    Oct 2019 - Dec 2019

    Project description: Utilized Kali Linux terminal and the Aircrack-ng suite along with an Alfa wireless adapter to implement an evil twin attack in a controlled environment. This involved spoofing a legitimate AP and sending deauthentication packets in order to get clients to reconnect to our wireless network, at which point they would become a lot more vulnerable, as their traffic could be potentially monitored or redirected by us.
